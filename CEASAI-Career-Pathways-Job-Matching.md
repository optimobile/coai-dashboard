## Part 3: Job Matching System

### 3.1 AI-Powered Job Matching Algorithm

The CEASA Job Matching System uses machine learning to match certified analysts with job opportunities based on skills, experience, preferences, and career goals.

#### **Matching Criteria:**

**Analyst Profile:**
- Certification tier (Fundamentals, Professional, Expert)
- Specialization tracks completed
- Years of experience
- Previous employers and roles
- Geographic location and preferences
- Salary expectations
- Career goals and aspirations
- Work preferences (remote, on-site, hybrid)
- Industry preferences
- Availability and timeline

**Job Profile:**
- Required certification tier
- Preferred specializations
- Years of experience required
- Geographic location
- Salary range
- Job type (full-time, contract, consulting)
- Industry and sector
- Company size and type
- Remote work options
- Timeline and urgency

#### **Matching Algorithm:**

**Tier Matching (Must-Have):**
- Job requires Professional tier → Match with Professional or Expert analysts
- Job requires Expert tier → Match with Expert analysts only
- Exact tier match preferred, but higher tier acceptable

**Specialization Matching (Weighted):**
- Primary specialization match: 40% weight
- Secondary specialization match: 20% weight
- Relevant experience: 20% weight
- Generalist capability: 20% weight

**Experience Matching (Weighted):**
- Years of experience: 30% weight
- Relevant industry experience: 25% weight
- Relevant role experience: 25% weight
- Company size experience: 20% weight

**Location Matching (Weighted):**
- Exact location match: 40% weight
- Regional match: 30% weight
- Remote work capability: 30% weight

**Preference Matching (Weighted):**
- Salary alignment: 20% weight
- Work type preference: 20% weight
- Industry preference: 20% weight
- Career goal alignment: 20% weight
- Timeline alignment: 20% weight

**Overall Match Score:**
- Calculated as weighted average of all criteria
- Score range: 0-100
- Match score >75 = Strong match
- Match score 50-75 = Moderate match
- Match score <50 = Weak match
