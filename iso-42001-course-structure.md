# ISO 42001 AI Management System - Course Structure

## Course Trilogy Overview

The ISO 42001 AI Management System training program provides comprehensive education on implementing and managing an Artificial Intelligence Management System (AIMS) according to the international standard ISO/IEC 42001:2023. The program is structured across three progressive levels to accommodate learners from foundational understanding through expert implementation.

---

## Course 1: ISO 42001 Fundamentals

**Target Audience**: AI practitioners, compliance officers, risk managers, product managers, and anyone involved in AI development or governance who needs foundational knowledge of the ISO 42001 standard.

**Duration**: 20 hours (8 modules)

**Prerequisites**: Basic understanding of AI/ML concepts and general management system principles (recommended but not required)

**Price Structure**:
- One-time payment: $499 USD
- 3-month plan: $199/month ($597 total)
- 6-month plan: $99/month ($594 total)
- 12-month plan: $59/month ($708 total)

### Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, learners will be able to:

1. Explain the purpose, scope, and benefits of ISO 42001
2. Understand the structure of an AI Management System (AIMS)
3. Identify the key clauses (4-10) and their requirements
4. Describe the Plan-Do-Check-Act (PDCA) methodology
5. Recognize AI-specific risks and ethical considerations
6. Understand the AI system lifecycle management approach
7. Explain how ISO 42001 aligns with other standards (ISO 27001, EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF)
8. Prepare for implementing an AIMS in their organization

### Module Structure

#### Module 1: Introduction to ISO 42001 (150 minutes)
**Learning Objectives**:
- Understand the history and development of ISO 42001
- Explain why AI governance requires a dedicated management system standard
- Identify the benefits of ISO 42001 certification
- Describe the structure of the standard (clauses 1-10)
- Recognize the applicability across industries and organization sizes

**Key Topics**:
- The need for AI governance
- ISO 42001 development timeline
- Relationship to ISO 27001 and other management system standards
- Certification process overview
- Real-world adoption examples (Microsoft, AWS)

**Assessment**: 10 multiple-choice questions

---

#### Module 2: Context of the Organization (Clause 4) (150 minutes)
**Learning Objectives**:
- Identify internal and external issues affecting the AIMS
- Determine interested parties and their requirements
- Define the scope of an AIMS
- Understand the AIMS framework and processes

**Key Topics**:
- Understanding organizational context for AI
- Stakeholder identification and analysis
- Scope definition considerations
- AIMS documentation requirements
- Practical examples of scope statements

**Assessment**: 10 multiple-choice questions

---

#### Module 3: Leadership and Governance (Clause 5) (150 minutes)
**Learning Objectives**:
- Explain leadership responsibilities in AIMS
- Develop an AI policy statement
- Define organizational roles and responsibilities
- Establish governance structures for AI

**Key Topics**:
- Top management commitment
- AI policy development
- Roles: AI Officer, Data Protection Officer, Ethics Committee
- Governance models for AI projects
- Integration with existing governance structures

**Assessment**: 10 multiple-choice questions

---

#### Module 4: Planning and Risk Assessment (Clause 6) (180 minutes)
**Learning Objectives**:
- Conduct AI risk assessments
- Set AI objectives aligned with organizational goals
- Perform AI system impact assessments
- Plan actions to address risks and opportunities

**Key Topics**:
- AI risk identification methodologies
- Risk assessment frameworks (ISO 31000, ISO 23894)
- AI system impact assessment (AISIA) process
- Setting measurable AI objectives
- Risk treatment strategies

**Assessment**: 10 multiple-choice questions

---

#### Module 5: Support and Resources (Clause 7) (150 minutes)
**Learning Objectives**:
- Identify resource requirements for AIMS
- Ensure competence and awareness of personnel
- Establish communication processes
- Manage documented information

**Key Topics**:
- Resource allocation for AI projects
- Competence requirements for AI roles
- Awareness training programs
- Internal and external communication
- Documentation and record-keeping

**Assessment**: 10 multiple-choice questions

---

#### Module 6: Operation and AI System Lifecycle (Clause 8) (180 minutes)
**Learning Objectives**:
- Implement operational controls for AI systems
- Manage the AI system lifecycle
- Ensure data governance and quality
- Oversee third-party AI relationships

**Key Topics**:
- Operational planning and control
- AI system lifecycle stages (design, development, deployment, monitoring, retirement)
- Data management and quality (ISO 5259)
- Third-party AI vendor management
- Change management for AI systems

**Assessment**: 10 multiple-choice questions

---

#### Module 7: Performance Evaluation (Clause 9) (150 minutes)
**Learning Objectives**:
- Monitor and measure AIMS performance
- Conduct internal AIMS audits
- Perform management reviews
- Analyze and evaluate AI system performance

**Key Topics**:
- Key performance indicators (KPIs) for AIMS
- Monitoring and measurement methods
- Internal audit planning and execution
- Management review process
- Continuous performance evaluation

**Assessment**: 10 multiple-choice questions

---

#### Module 8: Improvement and Certification (Clause 10) (150 minutes)
**Learning Objectives**:
- Implement continual improvement processes
- Handle nonconformities and corrective actions
- Prepare for ISO 42001 certification
- Understand the certification audit process

**Key Topics**:
- Continual improvement methodology
- Nonconformity identification and correction
- Corrective action procedures
- Certification preparation checklist
- Certification audit stages
- Post-certification maintenance

**Assessment**: 10 multiple-choice questions + Final Exam (30 questions)

## Course 2: ISO 42001 Advanced

**Target Audience**: AIMS implementers, internal auditors, compliance managers, and professionals responsible for establishing or maintaining an ISO 42001-compliant AIMS.

**Duration**: 30 hours (10 modules)

**Prerequisites**: Completion of ISO 42001 Fundamentals or equivalent knowledge

**Price Structure**:
- One-time payment: $799 USD
- 3-month plan: $299/month ($897 total)
- 6-month plan: $159/month ($954 total)
- 12-month plan: $89/month ($1,068 total)

### Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, learners will be able to:

1. Implement a complete AIMS from scratch
2. Conduct comprehensive AI risk assessments
3. Develop AIMS documentation (policies, procedures, records)
4. Perform internal AIMS audits
5. Integrate AIMS with ISO 27001 and ISO 27701
6. Align AIMS with EU AI Act and NIST AI RMF requirements
7. Manage AI system lifecycle across complex organizations
8. Prepare for third-party certification audits

### Module Structure

1. **Advanced AIMS Implementation** (180 minutes) - Gap analysis, implementation roadmap, resource planning
2. **AI Risk Management Deep Dive** (180 minutes) - Advanced risk assessment, bias detection, ethical risk analysis
3. **AIMS Documentation Development** (180 minutes) - Policy writing, procedure development, record templates
4. **AI System Impact Assessment (AISIA)** (180 minutes) - Detailed AISIA methodology, stakeholder engagement, impact evaluation
5. **Data Governance for AI** (180 minutes) - Data quality frameworks, data lineage, privacy-enhancing technologies
6. **Third-Party AI Management** (180 minutes) - Vendor assessment, contractual safeguards, ongoing monitoring
7. **Internal AIMS Auditing** (180 minutes) - Audit planning, execution, reporting, follow-up
8. **Cross-Framework Integration** (180 minutes) - Mapping ISO 42001 to EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, ISO 27001
9. **Performance Monitoring and KPIs** (180 minutes) - Defining metrics, dashboards, continuous monitoring
10. **Certification Preparation** (180 minutes) - Pre-audit readiness, documentation review, mock audits

**Assessment**: Module quizzes + Final Exam (50 questions) + Case Study Project
