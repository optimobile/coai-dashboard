## TC260 + PDCA Integration

### TC260 Framework Requirements
Based on TC260 AI Safety Governance Framework (September 2024):

**Core Principles:**
1. **Safety by Design** - AI systems must be designed with safety as primary consideration
2. **Human Oversight** - Humans must maintain ultimate control over AI decisions
3. **Transparency** - AI decisions must be explainable and auditable
4. **Accountability** - Clear responsibility chains for AI incidents
5. **Continuous Improvement** - Ongoing monitoring and refinement (PDCA)

**Training Requirements (Implied from Framework):**
- Understanding of AI risks across different domains
- Knowledge of regulatory requirements (EU AI Act, NIST, TC260)
- Practical incident analysis skills
- Bias detection and mitigation techniques
- Emergency response procedures

### PDCA Methodology Integration

**Every course follows PDCA structure:**

**PLAN Phase (Week 1):**
- Define learning objectives
- Assess current knowledge
- Set personal goals
- Review course roadmap

**DO Phase (Weeks 2-3):**
- Watch video lessons
- Complete readings
- Practice with case studies
- Participate in discussions

**CHECK Phase (Week 4):**
- Take module quizzes
- Review incorrect answers
- Identify knowledge gaps
- Seek clarification

**ACT Phase (Week 5):**
- Apply learning to real scenarios
- Complete final assessment
- Receive certification
- Plan next course

**Continuous Improvement Loop:**
- Course content updated based on analyst feedback
- New case studies added from real Watchdog reports
- Quiz questions refined based on common mistakes
- Certification standards raised as field matures
