## Part 1: Board Member Position Descriptions

### CEASAI Advisory Board Chair

**Position Title:** Chair, CEASAI Advisory Board

**Reporting:** CEASAI Executive Director

**Time Commitment:** 20-30 hours per month

**Compensation:** €10,000-15,000 annually

**Term:** 2 years (renewable for one additional term)

**Responsibilities:**

The Chair leads the CEASAI Advisory Board and serves as the primary external representative of the board. The Chair is responsible for ensuring effective governance, strategic guidance, and stakeholder engagement.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Preside over quarterly board meetings and annual strategic retreat
- Represent the board to CEASAI leadership and external stakeholders
- Ensure board effectiveness and governance compliance
- Lead curriculum voting procedures and strategic decisions
- Facilitate relationships with regulatory authorities and international organizations
- Provide strategic guidance on CEASAI's direction and priorities
- Ensure board diversity, independence, and impartiality

**Required Qualifications:**
- 15+ years experience in AI governance, regulation, or policy
- Demonstrated leadership in international organizations or government
- Deep knowledge of EU AI Act and global AI governance landscape
- Excellent communication and stakeholder engagement skills
- Commitment to CEASAI's mission of AI safety and public benefit

**Ideal Candidate Profile:**
- Former EU AI Act negotiator or European Commission official
- Former head of national AI agency or regulatory authority
- International AI governance expert with global network
- Thought leader in AI safety and governance
- Track record of building credible, independent institutions

**Time Commitment:** 10-15 hours per month

**Compensation:** €6,000-10,000 annually

**Term:** 3 years (renewable)

- Strong relationships with regulatory authorities

**Ideal Candidate Profile:**
