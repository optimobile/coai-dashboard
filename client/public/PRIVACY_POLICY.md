# CSOAI Platform - Privacy Policy
**Effective Date:** December 28, 2025

---

## 1. Introduction

CSOAI ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform, including all related applications, websites, and services (collectively, the "Service").

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide:

- Account registration information (name, email, password)
- Profile information (bio, photo, preferences)
- Course enrollment and progress data
- Exam answers and scores
- Payment information (processed by Stripe, not stored by us)
- Communication with support (emails, messages)
- User-generated content (posts, comments, submissions)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect:

- Device information (type, operating system, browser)
- Usage data (pages visited, time spent, clicks)
- IP address and location data
- Cookies and similar technologies
- Performance and error logs

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive:

- OAuth authentication data (from Manus)
- Payment information (from Stripe)
- Referral information (from referral partners)
- Public data (from publicly available sources)

## 6. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

### 6.1 Right to Access
You have the right to request a copy of your personal data we hold.

**How to Exercise:**
- Submit request to dpo@csoai.org
- Include proof of identity
- We respond within 30 days

### 6.2 Right to Rectification
You have the right to correct inaccurate data.

**How to Exercise:**
- Update information in account settings
- Or submit request to dpo@csoai.org
- We correct within 30 days

### 6.3 Right to Erasure (Right to Be Forgotten)
You have the right to request deletion of your data.

**How to Exercise:**
- Submit request to dpo@csoai.org
- Include reason for deletion
- We delete within 30 days
- Exceptions: legal obligations, legitimate interests

### 6.4 Right to Restrict Processing
You have the right to limit how we use your data.

**How to Exercise:**
- Submit request to dpo@csoai.org
- Specify which processing to restrict
- We comply within 30 days

### 6.5 Right to Object
You have the right to object to certain processing.

**How to Exercise:**
- Submit request to dpo@csoai.org
- Specify which processing to object to
- We stop processing unless we have compelling reasons

### 6.6 Right to Data Portability
You have the right to receive your data in a portable format.

**How to Exercise:**
- Submit request to dpo@csoai.org
- We provide data in structured format within 30 days
- You can transfer to another service

### 6.7 Right to Lodge a Complaint
You have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

**How to Exercise:**
- Visit www.ico.org.uk
- Submit complaint about our data processing
- ICO investigates and takes action

## 7. Data Security

### 7.1 Security Measures

We implement:

- Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Regular security audits
- Penetration testing
- Access controls and authentication
- Intrusion detection systems
- Regular backups

### 7.2 Data Breach Response

In case of a breach:

- We investigate immediately
- We notify affected users within 72 hours
- We notify ICO if required
- We provide guidance on protective measures
- We document the incident

### 7.3 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:

- Keeping your password confidential
- Logging out after use
- Not sharing account access
- Reporting suspicious activity

## 10. Data Retention

### 10.1 Retention Periods

We retain data for:

- **Active accounts:** Duration of account + 1 year
- **Inactive accounts:** 2 years after last login
- **Payment records:** 7 years (legal requirement)
- **Support tickets:** 3 years
- **Audit logs:** 2 years
- **Marketing data:** Until you unsubscribe

### 10.2 Deletion Process

Upon deletion:

- Data removed from active systems
- Data removed from backups within 30 days
- Deletion confirmed in writing
- Exceptions for legal requirements
