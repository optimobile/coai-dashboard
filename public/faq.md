# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is CSOAI?

CSOAI (Council of Safety of AI) is the global regulatory authority for AI Safety. We provide comprehensive compliance assessment, training, certification, and oversight for AI systems across the European Union, United States, China, and other regions.

Our mission is to ensure AI systems are safe, transparent, and aligned with human values while creating meaningful employment for AI Safety Analysts.

### What makes CSOAI different from other compliance platforms?

CSOAI is unique because we combine:

1. **33-Agent Byzantine Council** - AI agents from 5 providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Kimi, DeepSeek) vote on compliance decisions, ensuring impartial, unbiased assessments
2. **Human Analyst Oversight** - Every decision is reviewed by certified human analysts, ensuring human judgment remains central
3. **Multi-Framework Compliance** - We support EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, TC260, and ISO 42001 standards
4. **Job Creation** - We're creating meaningful careers for AI Safety Analysts at €45-150/hour
5. **Complete Transparency** - Public Watchdog database shows all reported AI safety incidents

### Is CSOAI affiliated with any AI companies?

No. CSOAI is completely independent from all AI vendors. We use AI agents from multiple providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Kimi, DeepSeek) to ensure no single vendor can influence our decisions.

### How is CSOAI funded?

CSOAI generates revenue through:
- Training and certification programs (€49-€1,999)
- Enterprise compliance audits (€5,000)
- Enterprise subscriptions (€2,000/month)
- API access for developers (€99-€999/month)

## Compliance & Enterprise

### How does CSOAI assess AI system compliance?

Our process involves:

1. **System Registration** - You provide AI system details (name, type, use case, risk level)
2. **Framework Selection** - Choose compliance frameworks (EU AI Act, NIST, TC260, ISO 42001)
3. **Assessment** - Our 33-Agent Council analyzes your system against framework requirements
4. **Report Generation** - Detailed compliance report with findings and recommendations
5. **Remediation** - We help you address compliance gaps
6. **Certification** - Once compliant, you receive CSOAI compliance certification

### How long does a compliance assessment take?

- **Quick Assessment**: 2-3 days (basic compliance check)
- **Standard Assessment**: 1-2 weeks (comprehensive review)
- **Enterprise Audit**: 4-8 weeks (in-depth analysis with on-site visits)

### What does a compliance assessment cost?

- **Quick Assessment**: €500
- **Standard Assessment**: €2,000
- **Enterprise Audit**: €5,000-€25,000 (depending on system complexity)
- **Ongoing Monitoring**: €2,000/month (continuous compliance tracking)

### Can CSOAI help us become compliant?

Yes! We offer:

- **Compliance Roadmap** - Step-by-step plan to achieve compliance
- **Remediation Support** - Help implementing required controls
- **Training** - Team training on compliance requirements
- **Ongoing Monitoring** - Continuous compliance tracking

### What is the 33-Agent Council?

The 33-Agent Council is our core innovation. It consists of:

- **33 AI agents** from 5 providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Kimi, DeepSeek)
- **Byzantine consensus voting** - Mathematically proven to be unbiased
- **Human analyst review** - Every decision reviewed by certified humans
- **Transparent decision-making** - All votes and reasoning are documented

This ensures compliance decisions are impartial and cannot be influenced by any single AI vendor or human bias.

### What is the Watchdog program?

The Watchdog program is our public incident reporting database. Anyone can:

- Report AI safety incidents anonymously
- View all reported incidents (anonymized)
- Contribute to AI safety research
- Help identify patterns and trends

All reports are analyzed by our 33-Agent Council and used to improve our compliance frameworks.

### How is my data protected?

We implement enterprise-grade security:

- **Encryption** - All data encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Controls** - Role-based access control with audit logging
- **Backups** - Daily encrypted backups to secure locations
- **Compliance** - GDPR, EU AI Act, ISO 27001 compliant
- **Transparency** - Public audit logs available to regulators
