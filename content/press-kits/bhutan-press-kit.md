**CSOAI Partners with Bhutan to Launch National AI Safety Framework**

**THIMPHU, Bhutan – [Date]** – The Council of Safe AI (CSOAI) today announced a landmark partnership with the Royal Government of Bhutan to develop and implement a national AI Safety Framework. This initiative will position Bhutan as a regional leader in ethical AI governance and will be aligned with the country's unique development philosophy of Gross National Happiness (GNH). The framework will address the AI governance gap and provide a clear roadmap for the safe and responsible adoption of AI technologies across various sectors of the Bhutanese economy.

“We are honored to partner with the Royal Government of Bhutan on this critical initiative,” said [Spokesperson Name], [Title] at CSOAI. “By proactively establishing a robust AI safety framework, Bhutan is not only safeguarding its citizens but also setting a global example for responsible AI innovation. Our framework is designed to be adaptable and to evolve with the needs of the Bhutanese people, ensuring that AI development always serves the greater good.”

[Optional: Quote from a Bhutanese government official, e.g., “This partnership with CSOAI is a significant step forward in our journey to become a digitally empowered nation,” said [Official’s Name], [Title]. “The AI Safety Framework will provide us with the necessary tools and expertise to harness the power of AI for sustainable development while preserving our unique cultural heritage.”]

The partnership will include the deployment of CSOAI’s white-label AI Safety Framework, the establishment of a 33-Agent Byzantine Council for AI oversight, and the training of 1,000 Bhutanese AI Safety Analysts through the CEASAI certification program. This comprehensive approach will build local capacity and create a self-sustaining ecosystem for AI governance in Bhutan.
