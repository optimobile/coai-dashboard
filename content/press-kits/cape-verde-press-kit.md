**PRAIA, CAPE VERDE – [Date]** – The Council of Safe AI (CSOAI) today announced a proposal to partner with the government of Cape Verde to implement a national AI Safety Framework. “Cape Verde has a unique opportunity to become a leader in AI governance in Africa,” said [Name], [Title] at CSOAI. “Our white-label AI Safety Framework, combined with our CEASAI certification program, will empower Cape Verde to build a safe and prosperous digital future.”

*   A customizable, white-label AI Safety Framework compliant with international standards.
*   The Certified Expert in AI Safety and Auditing (CEASAI) program to train and certify 1,000 local AI safety analysts.
*   The establishment of a 33-Agent Byzantine Council for AI to ensure robust and impartial oversight.

## Key Messages

### AI Challenges in Cape Verde
- Limited digital infrastructure in some areas, which could hinder the widespread adoption of AI technologies.
- A shortage of skilled human capital in AI and related fields, which is crucial for developing and managing AI systems.
- The absence of a formal AI governance framework and technology-specific regulations, which creates uncertainty and risk.
- The opportunity to leverage AI to boost key sectors like tourism, agriculture, and financial services.
- The potential to become a regional leader in AI governance and innovation, given the strong government commitment to digital transformation.
