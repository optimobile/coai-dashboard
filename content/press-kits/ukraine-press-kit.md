## Key Messages

### AI Challenges in Ukraine
- **Opportunity:** Ukraine's rapid adoption of AI in defense and public services, and its ambition to be a global AI leader, presents a significant opportunity for economic growth and technological advancement.
- **Challenge:** The lack of a comprehensive AI governance framework poses risks to citizens' rights, and could hinder innovation and economic competitiveness.
- **Opportunity:** The development of a national AI strategy and the creation of AI-focused initiatives like the AI Factory show a strong government commitment to building a robust AI ecosystem.
- **Challenge:** The ongoing war with Russia creates a unique and challenging environment for developing and implementing AI technologies and regulations.
- **Opportunity:** Leveraging open-source technologies and training smaller, specialized AI models can help Ukraine to innovate quickly and cost-effectively.
