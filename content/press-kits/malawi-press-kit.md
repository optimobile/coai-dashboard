## Key Messages

### AI Challenges in Malawi
- Limited access to technology and a significant digital divide, hindering widespread AI adoption.
- The absence of a formal AI governance framework, which creates risks of misuse and ethical breaches.
- A shortage of local AI talent and expertise, which is needed to develop and manage AI systems.
- The potential for algorithmic bias to perpetuate and amplify existing social inequalities.
