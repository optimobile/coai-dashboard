## Key Messages

### AI Challenges in Côte d'Ivoire
*   **Infrastructure Gap:** Limited access to reliable and affordable internet and electricity, especially in rural areas, hinders widespread AI adoption.
*   **Skills Shortage:** A lack of skilled AI professionals, data scientists, and engineers to develop and maintain AI systems.
*   **Data Scarcity and Quality:** Difficulty in accessing high-quality, localized data for training AI models, which can lead to biased or inaccurate results.
*   **Ethical and Social Concerns:** The potential for AI to exacerbate existing inequalities, job displacement, and the need for robust data privacy and protection measures.
