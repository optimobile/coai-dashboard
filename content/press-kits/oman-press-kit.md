**Muscat, Oman – [Date]** – The Sultanate of Oman today announced a landmark partnership with the Council of Safe AI (CSOAI) to develop and implement a national AI safety framework. The framework includes the establishment of a 33-Agent Byzantine Council, a decentralized oversight body, and the rollout of the SOAI-PDCA compliance methodology.
