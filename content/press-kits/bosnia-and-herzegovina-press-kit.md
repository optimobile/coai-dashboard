## Key Messages

### AI Challenges in Bosnia and Herzegovina
*   **Lack of a dedicated AI legal framework:** This creates uncertainty for businesses and investors, and poses risks to citizens' rights.
*   **Risk of a deepening digital divide:** Without a strategic approach to AI, there is a risk of exacerbating existing inequalities in society.
*   **Potential for inconsistent regulations:** The complex political structure of Bosnia and Herzegovina could lead to fragmented and inconsistent AI policies at different levels of government.
*   **Need for specialized skills:** While there is a growing talent pool, there is a need to develop specialized skills in AI safety, ethics, and governance.
