**Council of Safe AI (CSOAI) Proposes National AI Safety Framework for Jordan**

**AMMAN, Jordan – [Date]** – The Council of Safe AI (CSOAI), a leading global provider of AI safety and compliance solutions, today announced a proposal to partner with the government of Jordan to implement a national AI Safety Framework. The proposed framework is designed to help Jordan navigate the complexities of AI governance and to establish itself as a regional leader in responsible AI innovation.

“Jordan is at a critical juncture in its digital transformation journey,” said [Name], CEO of CSOAI. “The rapid adoption of AI presents a tremendous opportunity for economic growth and social progress, but it also highlights the urgent need for a comprehensive AI safety framework. CSOAI is committed to working with our partners in Jordan to build a secure and prosperous digital future for all.”

The proposed framework includes a white-label, adaptable AI Safety Framework, the CEASAI Certification program for local professionals, a 33-Agent Byzantine Council for AI oversight, and the SOAI-PDCA compliance methodology. As part of its commitment to building a global community of AI safety experts, CSOAI is also offering a £1 billion training giveaway, which will provide free CEASAI certification to 500,250 analysts worldwide, including citizens of Jordan.

“The CEASAI certification program is a game-changer for Jordan,” said [Name], Director of Government Affairs at CSOAI. “By creating a new generation of AI safety experts, we are not only building a more secure digital ecosystem, but we are also creating new economic opportunities and empowering individuals to participate in the AI revolution.”

CSOAI is calling on Jordanian government officials, business leaders, and civil society organizations to join in a national dialogue on the future of AI governance in Jordan. For more information about the proposed AI Safety Framework, please visit [website address].
