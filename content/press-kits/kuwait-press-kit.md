“Kuwait is at a pivotal moment in its AI journey. *   A **customizable AI Safety Framework** that aligns with international standards while respecting Kuwait’s unique cultural and legal context.
*   The **CEASAI (Certified Expert in AI Safety Auditing and Implementation) certification program** to train and certify a local workforce of AI safety professionals.
*   A **£1 Billion Global Training Giveaway**, offering free CEASAI certification to 500,250 individuals worldwide, including in Kuwait, to rapidly build local capacity.
