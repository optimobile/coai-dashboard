**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**CSOAI Launches Initiative to Establish AI Safety Framework in Afghanistan**

**KABUL, Afghanistan – [Date]** – The Council of Safe AI (CSOAI) today announced a major initiative to support the development and implementation of a national AI Safety Framework in Afghanistan. This initiative aims to provide the Afghan government and private sector with the tools and expertise needed to navigate the complexities of AI governance and to ensure that AI technologies are used in a safe, ethical, and beneficial manner.

"Afghanistan has a unique opportunity to build a future-proof AI ecosystem that drives economic growth and social progress while safeguarding the rights and interests of its citizens," said [CSOAI SPOKESPERSON NAME], [TITLE] at CSOAI. "Our white-label AI Safety Framework, combined with our comprehensive training and certification programs, will empower Afghanistan to become a leader in responsible AI innovation."

The CSOAI initiative includes:

*   **A White-Label AI Safety Framework:** A customizable and adaptable governance structure that is aligned with international best practices.
*   **CEASAI Certification:** A training and certification program to create a local pool of skilled AI Safety Analysts.
*   **The 33-Agent Byzantine Council:** A multi-layered and resilient oversight mechanism to ensure the fair and impartial implementation of the AI Safety Framework.
*   **The £1 Billion Training Giveaway:** A global initiative providing free access to the CEASAI certification, with a significant number of seats allocated for Afghan citizens.

CSOAI is committed to working closely with all stakeholders in Afghanistan to ensure the successful implementation of this initiative. The organization will be hosting a series of workshops and consultations in the coming months to engage with government officials, business leaders, academics, and civil society organizations.

**About CSOAI:**

The Council of Safe AI (CSOAI) is a global organization dedicated to promoting the safe and ethical development of artificial intelligence. We provide governments, businesses, and individuals with the tools, expertise, and resources they need to navigate the complex challenges of AI governance. Our mission is to ensure that AI is used for the benefit of all humanity.

## Key Messages

### AI Challenges in Afghanistan
- Limited digital infrastructure and a shortage of skilled AI professionals.
- The absence of a national AI strategy and regulatory framework creates uncertainty and risk.
- The potential for misuse of AI for surveillance and the exacerbation of social inequalities.
- Opportunities for AI-driven growth in sectors like mining, telecommunications, and humanitarian aid.
- The potential to create a new generation of tech-savvy professionals through AI education and training.
