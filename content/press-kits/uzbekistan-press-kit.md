**CSOAI Launches Initiative to Bring AI Safety Framework to Uzbekistan**

**[City, State, Date]** – The Council of Safe AI (CSOAI) today announced a major initiative to support Uzbekistan in developing a national AI safety framework. This move comes as the country rapidly expands its digital economy and AI adoption across key sectors. CSOAI will offer its white-label AI Safety Framework, professional training, and a novel AI-powered oversight system to help Uzbekistan build a responsible and ethical AI ecosystem.

"Uzbekistan is at a pivotal moment in its technological development," said [CSOAI Spokesperson Name], [Title] at CSOAI. "By proactively establishing a robust AI governance framework, Uzbekistan can ensure that AI is a force for good, driving economic growth and improving the lives of its citizens. We are proud to partner with Uzbekistan on this important journey."

The CSOAI initiative will provide Uzbekistan with the tools and expertise needed to navigate the complexities of AI governance. This includes the CEASAI Certification program, which will train and certify local AI safety analysts, and the 33-Agent Byzantine Council, a decentralized AI oversight system.

CSOAI is also offering a £1 billion training giveaway, providing free access to its CEASAI certification program to the citizens of Uzbekistan. This program aims to train 500,250 analysts globally, creating a skilled workforce to support the implementation of AI safety frameworks worldwide.
