**CSOAI Partners with Guinea to Launch National AI Safety Framework**

**CONAKRY, Guinea – [Date]** – The Council of Safe AI (CSOAI) is pleased to announce a strategic partnership with the Republic of Guinea to develop and implement a national AI Safety Framework. This landmark initiative will position Guinea as a regional leader in responsible AI governance and prepare the nation for the upcoming EU AI Act deadline.

“We are thrilled to partner with Guinea on this critical initiative,” said [CSOAI Spokesperson Name], [Title] at CSOAI. “By proactively establishing a robust AI safety framework, Guinea is not only mitigating potential risks but also unlocking the immense potential of AI to drive economic growth and social progress.”

The partnership will involve the deployment of CSOAI’s white-label AI Safety Framework, customized to meet the specific needs of Guinea. The framework will be supported by the CEASAI Certification program, which will train and certify 1,000 AI Safety Analysts in Guinea, creating a skilled workforce to oversee the implementation of the framework.

“This partnership is a testament to Guinea’s commitment to embracing the fourth industrial revolution,” said [Guinean Government Official Name], [Title]. “We are confident that with CSOAI’s expertise, we can build a future where AI is harnessed safely, ethically, and for the benefit of all Guineans.”
