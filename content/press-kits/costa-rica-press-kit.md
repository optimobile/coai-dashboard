**CSOAI Partners with Costa Rica to Launch National AI Safety Framework**

**SAN JOSÉ, COSTA RICA – [Date]** – The Council of Safe AI (CSOAI) today announced a landmark partnership with the government of Costa Rica to implement a national AI Safety Framework. This initiative, which includes a £1 billion global training giveaway, will position Costa Rica as a leader in responsible AI development and governance in Latin America.

The AI Safety Framework is a comprehensive, turnkey solution that will provide Costa Rica with a robust governance structure for the development and deployment of artificial intelligence. The framework is designed to be customized to meet the specific needs of the nation and will be implemented in a phased approach over the next year.

“We are thrilled to partner with Costa Rica on this historic initiative,” said [CSOAI Spokesperson Name], [Title] at the CSOAI. “Costa Rica has already demonstrated its commitment to responsible innovation, and we are confident that our AI Safety Framework will help the nation to build a safe, ethical, and prosperous AI future.”

As part of the partnership, the CSOAI will provide free access to its Certified Expert in AI Safety and Audit (CEASAI) program to Costa Rican citizens. The goal is to train 1,000 AI safety analysts in the country, creating a skilled workforce that can support the implementation of the national AI framework.

“This is a transformative opportunity for Costa Rica,” said [Costa Rican Government Official Name], [Title]. “The CSOAI’s AI Safety Framework and the CEASAI training program will provide us with the tools and the talent we need to become a global leader in responsible AI.”
