**CSOAI Launches Initiative to Establish AI Safety Framework in North Macedonia**

**SKOPJE, North Macedonia – [Date]** – The Council of Safe AI (CSOAI) today announced a major initiative to support North Macedonia in establishing a national AI Safety Framework. This initiative aims to provide the country with the tools, resources, and expertise needed to navigate the complexities of AI governance and ensure the responsible development and deployment of AI technologies.

With the European Union's AI Act deadline of February 2, 2026, approaching, CSOAI's initiative comes at a critical time for North Macedonia. The proposed framework will help the country align with international best practices, mitigate the risks associated with AI, and foster an environment of trust and innovation.

"We believe that a robust AI safety framework is essential for any country that wants to harness the full potential of AI," said [Name], [Title] at CSOAI. "Our goal is to empower North Macedonia to become a regional leader in responsible AI, and we are committed to providing the support and resources needed to achieve this vision."

CSOAI's initiative includes a comprehensive, white-label AI Safety Framework that can be tailored to the specific needs of North Macedonia. The initiative also includes the CEASAI Certification program, which will provide free training to 1,000 citizens of North Macedonia to become certified AI Safety Analysts.

**About CSOAI:**

The Council of Safe AI (CSOAI) is a global organization dedicated to promoting the safe and responsible development of artificial intelligence. We provide governments, businesses, and other organizations with the tools, resources, and expertise they need to navigate the complexities of AI governance.

### AI Challenges in North Macedonia
- Lack of specific AI regulations and a national AI strategy.
- Shortage of skilled AI professionals due to emigration (brain drain).
- Insufficient high-quality data for training and developing AI models.
- Need for greater public awareness and trust in AI technologies.
