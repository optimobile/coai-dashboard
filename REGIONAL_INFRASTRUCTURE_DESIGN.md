# Regional Infrastructure Design

## Overview

COAI operates a **three-tier hierarchy** in each region:
1. **Regional Specialist** - Oversees entire region, manages industry specialists
2. **Industry Specialists** - Manage analysts within specific industries (healthcare, finance, etc.)
3. **Certified Analysts** - Perform watchdog report reviews and compliance assessments

---

## Tier 1: Regional Specialists

### Responsibilities:
- Oversee all analysts and industry specialists in their region
- Ensure compliance with regional regulations (EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, etc.)
- Review escalated cases from industry specialists
- Train and certify new industry specialists
- Maintain relationships with regional regulators
- Adapt global COAI standards to regional requirements

### Qualifications:
- 5+ years AI/ML or regulatory compliance experience
- Legal/regulatory background in target region
- Industry certifications (CIPP/E, CISSP, NIST AI RMF Certified, etc.)
- Completed all COAI regional courses with 95%+ scores
- Passed COAI Regional Specialist Certification Exam

### Compensation:
- Base salary: $5,000-$10,000/month (depending on region size)
- Performance bonus: 5% of total regional analyst earnings
- Course commissions: 10% of all course sales in region
- **Estimated Annual:** $80K-$150K

### Metrics:
- Number of analysts supervised (target: 100-500)
- Regional compliance score (target: 95%+)
- Analyst quality score (target: 90%+)
- Course completion rate (target: 85%+)

---

## Tier 2: Industry Specialists

### Responsibilities:
- Manage 10-50 analysts within specific industry (healthcare, finance, automotive, etc.)
- Assign watchdog reports to analysts based on expertise
- Review analyst work and provide feedback
- Escalate complex cases to regional specialist or council
- Train analysts on industry-specific regulations
- Maintain industry expertise and certifications

### Qualifications:
- 3+ years experience in target industry
- Completed COAI regional compliance bundle
- Completed industry-specific training modules
- Passed COAI Industry Specialist Certification Exam
- Supervised by regional specialist for 3-month probation

### Compensation:
- Base salary: $3,000-$5,000/month
- Performance bonus: 10% of supervised analyst earnings
- Course commissions: 20% of course sales to supervised analysts
- **Estimated Annual:** $50K-$80K

### Metrics:
- Number of analysts supervised (target: 10-50)
- Analyst retention rate (target: 90%+)
- Analyst quality score (target: 85%+)
- Report turnaround time (target: <24 hours)

---

## Tier 3: Certified Analysts

### Responsibilities:
- Review watchdog reports assigned by industry specialist
- Conduct compliance assessments for AI systems
- Participate in PDCA cycles for continuous improvement
- Maintain certifications and complete ongoing training
- Escalate complex cases to industry specialist

### Qualifications:
- Completed COAI regional compliance bundle
- Passed COAI Analyst Certification Exam (region-specific)
- Assigned to industry specialist for supervision

### Compensation:
- Per-report payment: $15-$50 per report (based on complexity)
- Average earnings: $45/hour
- Top performers: $60-$80/hour
- **Estimated Annual:** $30K-$60K (part-time to full-time)

### Metrics:
- Reports reviewed per week (target: 20-40)
- Quality score (target: 85%+)
- Turnaround time (target: <4 hours per report)
- Certification status (must maintain active certifications)

## Workflow: Report Assignment

### Step 1: Report Submission
- User submits watchdog report or AI system for assessment
- System auto-detects region based on:
  - User location (IP address)
  - AI system deployment location
  - Applicable regulations selected

### Step 2: Regional Routing
- Report routed to appropriate regional specialist
- Regional specialist reviews and assigns to industry specialist
- Industry specialist assigns to certified analyst

### Step 3: Analyst Review
- Analyst reviews report within 4 hours
- Analyst completes assessment using region-specific framework
- Analyst submits findings to industry specialist

### Step 4: Specialist Quality Check
- Industry specialist reviews analyst work
- Provides feedback and quality score
- Approves or requests revisions

### Step 5: Escalation (if needed)
- Complex cases escalated to regional specialist
- High-risk cases escalated to 33-Agent Council
- Council makes final decision with Byzantine consensus

### Step 6: Resolution
- Final decision communicated to user
- Report published to public dashboard (if approved)
- PDCA cycle initiated for continuous improvement

- **Work Queue:** Assigned reports awaiting review

## Quality Assurance System

### Analyst Quality Scoring:
- **Accuracy:** Does assessment match specialist review? (40% weight)
- **Completeness:** Are all required fields filled? (20% weight)
- **Timeliness:** Was report completed within SLA? (20% weight)
- **Clarity:** Is reasoning clear and well-documented? (20% weight)

### Specialist Quality Scoring:
- **Analyst Performance:** Average quality score of supervised analysts (40% weight)
- **Turnaround Time:** How quickly are reports reviewed? (20% weight)
- **Escalation Rate:** How often are cases escalated to regional specialist? (20% weight)
- **Analyst Retention:** Do analysts stay or leave? (20% weight)

### Regional Quality Scoring:
- **Compliance Rate:** % of AI systems meeting regional standards (40% weight)
- **Incident Resolution:** How quickly are incidents resolved? (20% weight)
- **Analyst Satisfaction:** Analyst retention and feedback scores (20% weight)
- **Regulator Feedback:** Feedback from regional regulators (20% weight)
