# CEASA Continuing Education & Certificate Renewal System

**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** December 27, 2025  
**Prepared by:** Manus AI  
**Purpose:** Comprehensive framework for continuing education requirements and certificate renewal procedures

---

## Executive Summary

The CEASA Continuing Education (CE) and Certificate Renewal System ensures that certified AI Safety Analysts maintain current knowledge, skills, and competence throughout their careers. By requiring ongoing professional development, CEASA maintains the credibility and value of its certifications while supporting analysts in staying current with rapidly evolving AI safety landscape.

**Certificate Validity Periods:** 3 years (all tiers)  
**Continuing Education Requirements:** 30-60 CEUs per 3-year period (depending on tier)  
**Renewal Process:** Online application + CEU verification + renewal fee  
**Renewal Deadline:** 90 days before certificate expiration  

---

## Part 1: Continuing Education Requirements

### 1.1 CEU (Continuing Education Unit) Definition

**One CEU = One Hour of Approved Professional Development**

A Continuing Education Unit represents one hour of participation in approved professional development activities that maintain or enhance competence in AI safety, governance, or related fields.

**CEU Approval Criteria:**
- Directly relevant to AI safety, governance, or compliance
- Delivered by qualified instructors or experts
- Includes learning objectives and assessment
- Documented with completion certificates
- Meets ISO 17024 continuing education standards

### 1.2 Continuing Education Requirements by Tier

#### **Fundamentals Tier Certificate Renewal (3-year period)**

**Total CEUs Required:** 30 CEUs per 3-year period (10 CEUs per year)

**Breakdown:**
- **CEASA-Approved Providers:** 15 CEUs minimum (50%)
- **Industry-Approved Providers:** 15 CEUs maximum (50%)

**Renewal Timeline:**
- Certificate valid for 3 years from issuance
- Renewal application opens 90 days before expiration
- Renewal deadline: 30 days before expiration
- Late renewal: Additional $50 fee + 30-day extension

#### **Professional Tier Certificate Renewal (3-year period)**

**Total CEUs Required:** 45 CEUs per 3-year period (15 CEUs per year)

**Breakdown:**
- **CEASA-Approved Providers:** 27 CEUs minimum (60%)
- **Industry-Approved Providers:** 18 CEUs maximum (40%)
- **Advanced Requirements:** Minimum 1 case study, research project, or publication per year

**Renewal Timeline:**
- Certificate valid for 3 years from issuance
- Renewal application opens 90 days before expiration
- Renewal deadline: 30 days before expiration
- Late renewal: Additional $75 fee + 30-day extension

#### **Expert Tier Certificate Renewal (3-year period)**

**Total CEUs Required:** 60 CEUs per 3-year period (20 CEUs per year)

**Breakdown:**
- **CEASA-Approved Providers:** 42 CEUs minimum (70%)
- **Industry-Approved Providers:** 18 CEUs maximum (30%)
- **Advanced Requirements:** Minimum 2 case studies, research projects, publications, or governance activities per year

**Renewal Timeline:**
- Certificate valid for 3 years from issuance
- Renewal application opens 90 days before expiration
- Renewal deadline: 30 days before expiration
- Late renewal: Additional $100 fee + 30-day extension

#### **Specialization Track Certificates (3-year period)**

**Total CEUs Required:** 15 CEUs per 3-year period (5 CEUs per year)

**Breakdown:**
- **CEASA-Approved Providers:** 9 CEUs minimum (60%)
- **Industry-Approved Providers:** 6 CEUs maximum (40%)

**Renewal Timeline:**
- Same as primary tier certificate
- Can be renewed independently or with primary tier
- Separate renewal fee ($25 per specialization)

## Part 2: Certificate Renewal Process

### 2.1 Renewal Timeline & Deadlines

**Certificate Lifecycle:**
- **Issuance Date:** Certificate issued upon passing exam
- **Validity Period:** 3 years from issuance
- **Renewal Window Opens:** 90 days before expiration
- **Renewal Deadline:** 30 days before expiration
- **Grace Period:** 30 days after expiration (with additional fee)
- **Expiration:** Certificate becomes invalid after grace period

**Renewal Notifications:**
- **90 Days Before:** Email notification with renewal instructions
- **60 Days Before:** Reminder email with CEU tracking link
- **30 Days Before:** Final deadline notification
- **15 Days After Expiration:** Notification of late renewal option
- **30 Days After Expiration:** Certificate expires permanently

### 2.2 Renewal Application Process

**Step 1: Online Application (Renewal Portal)**
- [ ] Log into CEASA renewal portal
- [ ] Verify personal information
- [ ] Review CEU requirements for tier
- [ ] Confirm CEU completion
- [ ] Agree to renewal terms and conditions
- [ ] Submit application

**Step 2: CEU Verification**
- [ ] Analyst uploads CEU documentation
- [ ] CEASA staff verifies CEU eligibility
- [ ] CEU hours counted toward requirement
- [ ] Verification completed within 5 business days
- [ ] Analyst notified of verification results

**Step 3: CEU Sufficiency Check**
- [ ] CEASA verifies total CEUs meet requirement
- [ ] Analyst notified if CEUs insufficient
- [ ] Analyst has 15 days to complete additional CEUs
- [ ] CEU requirement met or renewal denied

**Step 4: Renewal Fee Payment**
- [ ] Renewal fee charged based on tier
- [ ] Payment options: Credit card, bank transfer, check
- [ ] Payment processed securely
- [ ] Confirmation email sent

**Step 5: Certificate Renewal**
- [ ] New certificate generated
- [ ] Certificate issued with new expiration date
- [ ] Certificate sent via email (PDF) and mail (physical)
- [ ] Analyst notified of renewal completion
- [ ] Certificate verification updated in public database

### 2.3 Renewal Fees

| Tier | Renewal Fee | Late Renewal Fee | Specialization Fee |
|------|-------------|------------------|-------------------|
| **Fundamentals** | $100 | $150 | N/A |
| **Professional** | $150 | $225 | $25 per specialization |
| **Expert** | $200 | $300 | $25 per specialization |

**Fee Waivers & Discounts:**
- [ ] Hardship waiver (50% discount) for financial hardship
- [ ] Group renewal discount (10% for 5+ analysts)
- [ ] Early renewal discount (10% if renewed 6+ months early)
- [ ] Non-profit organization discount (25%)
- [ ] Student/academic discount (25%)

### 2.4 Renewal Denial & Appeals

**Grounds for Renewal Denial:**
- Insufficient CEUs (not meeting requirement)
- Non-payment of renewal fee
- Ethical violations or misconduct
- Fraudulent CEU documentation
- Failure to comply with CEASA standards

**Renewal Denial Process:**
- [ ] CEASA notifies analyst of denial reason
- [ ] Analyst has 15 days to respond
- [ ] CEASA reviews response
- [ ] Final decision communicated within 30 days
- [ ] Analyst may appeal to CEASA board

**Appeals Process:**
- [ ] Analyst submits written appeal within 30 days
- [ ] Appeals committee reviews appeal
- [ ] Analyst may provide additional documentation
- [ ] Committee decision within 30 days
- [ ] Final decision is binding

## Part 4: Specialization Track Renewal

### 4.1 Specialization Certificate Renewal

**Specialization Certificate Validity:**
- Valid for 3 years from issuance
- Can be renewed independently or with primary tier
- Same renewal process as primary tier
- Separate renewal fee ($25 per specialization)

**Specialization CEU Requirements:**
- 15 CEUs per 3-year period (5 CEUs per year)

## Part 5: Advanced Renewal Options

### 5.1 Early Renewal

**Early Renewal Benefits:**
- 10% discount on renewal fee
- Extends certificate validity by 3 years
- Allows analysts to renew early if desired
- Useful for analysts changing jobs or roles

**Early Renewal Eligibility:**
- Certificate must be valid (not expired)
- CEU requirement must be met
- Minimum 6 months before expiration
- Early renewal fee applies

**Early Renewal Process:**
- Same as standard renewal process
- Analyst initiates early renewal in portal
- CEU verification and fee payment
- New certificate issued with extended validity

### 5.2 Inactive Status

**Inactive Certificate Status:**
- Analysts can place certificate on inactive status
- Certificate remains valid but not actively used
- No CEU requirement during inactive period
- Reactivation requires CEU completion

**Inactive Status Process:**
- [ ] Analyst requests inactive status
- [ ] Certificate placed on inactive status
- [ ] Public directory shows inactive status
- [ ] No renewal fees during inactive period
- [ ] Reactivation requires current CEUs

**Reactivation Process:**
- [ ] Analyst requests reactivation
- [ ] CEU requirement must be met (based on time inactive)
- [ ] Renewal fee applies
- [ ] Certificate reactivated and renewed
- [ ] Public directory updated

### 5.3 Reciprocal Recognition

**Recognition of Other Certifications:**
- CISSP, PMP, and other professional certifications recognized
- Equivalent CEU credit for related certifications
- Mutual recognition agreements with other bodies
- Streamlined renewal for analysts with multiple certifications

**Reciprocal Agreement Process:**
- [ ] CEASA establishes agreements with other bodies
- [ ] Defines equivalent CEU credit
- [ ] Develops verification procedures
- [ ] Analysts can apply for CEU credit
- [ ] Streamlined renewal process
