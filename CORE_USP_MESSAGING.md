**Primary Goal:** Capture 10,000 LOI signups

**LOI Form Fields:**
- Name
- Email
- Role (Individual/Enterprise/Government)
- Interest (Training/Compliance/Oversight)
- Country

**LOI Incentive:**
- "Founding Member Discount: 50% off first year"
- "Early Access to Certification Program"

**LOI Placement:**
