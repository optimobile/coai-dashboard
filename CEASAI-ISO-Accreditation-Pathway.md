## Part 1: ISO/IEC 17024 - Personnel Certification Body Accreditation

### 1.1 Standard Overview

**ISO/IEC 17024:2012 - Conformity Assessment - General Requirements for Bodies Operating Certification of Persons**

This standard specifies principles and requirements for organizations certifying individuals against specific competency requirements. It ensures that personnel certification bodies operate with impartiality, competence, and integrity.

### 1.2 Key Requirements

#### 1.2.1 Organizational Requirements

**Independence and Impartiality**
- Organizational independence: Certification body must be independent from training providers
- Financial independence: No financial incentives for passing or failing candidates
- Conflict of interest: Clear procedures to identify and manage conflicts
- Impartiality policy: Documented policy ensuring impartial certification

**Competence and Resources**
- Personnel competence: Certification staff must have relevant expertise
- Training and development: Ongoing training for certification staff
- Resource allocation: Sufficient resources for certification operations
- Infrastructure: Appropriate facilities and technology

**Management System**
- Quality management: Documented quality management system
- Procedures: Documented procedures for all certification activities
- Documentation: Comprehensive documentation of all processes
- Records: Secure record-keeping and retention procedures

#### 1.2.2 Certification Scheme Requirements

**Scheme Development**
- Competency standards: Clear definition of competency standards
- Assessment methods: Documented assessment methods and procedures
- Pass/fail criteria: Clear criteria for certification decisions
- Validity period: Defined validity period for certificates
- Recertification: Requirements for recertification

**Assessment and Examination**
- Assessment design: Assessments aligned with competency standards
- Examination security: Procedures to ensure examination integrity
- Proctoring: Procedures for examination proctoring
- Grading: Clear grading and scoring procedures
- Appeal: Appeal procedures for assessment decisions

**Candidate Management**
- Application procedures: Clear application procedures
- Eligibility criteria: Defined eligibility criteria
- Candidate support: Support materials and resources
- Confidentiality: Protection of candidate information
- Non-discrimination: Non-discriminatory assessment procedures

#### 1.2.3 Certificate Management

**Certificate Issuance**
- Certificate content: Required information on certificates
- Certificate format: Standardized certificate format
- Certificate security: Security features to prevent counterfeiting
- Certificate delivery: Procedures for certificate delivery
- Certificate registry: Publicly accessible certificate registry

**Certificate Validity and Maintenance**
- Validity period: Defined certificate validity period
- Maintenance requirements: Requirements for certificate maintenance
- Recertification: Recertification procedures and timing
- Suspension/revocation: Procedures for certificate suspension or revocation
- Appeal: Appeal procedures for suspension/revocation

#### 1.2.4 Quality Assurance

**Internal Audits**
- Audit frequency: Regular internal audits (minimum annually)
- Audit scope: Comprehensive audit of all certification activities
- Audit procedures: Documented audit procedures
- Audit findings: Documentation of findings and corrective actions
- Audit follow-up: Follow-up on corrective actions

**Monitoring and Evaluation**
- Performance monitoring: Monitoring of certification performance
- Candidate feedback: Collection and analysis of candidate feedback
- Examiner evaluation: Evaluation of examiner performance
- Continuous improvement: Procedures for continuous improvement

**External Audits**
- Accreditation body audit: Audit by accreditation body
- Audit frequency: Regular audits (typically annually)
- Audit cooperation: Full cooperation with accreditation auditors
- Audit findings: Response to audit findings
- Corrective actions: Implementation of corrective actions
